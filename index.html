Developer Quickstart

Get Toro Running in 2 Minutes

Setting up Toro locally is straightforward. Grab the binary, configure your keys, and activate.

1

Download Release Binary

Download the standalone Toro.exe from the releases page and save it in a folder of your choice.

2

Windows SmartScreen (First Run)

Because Toro is a new application, Windows might show a blue "Windows protected your PC" popup. Toro is completely open-source and 100% safe to use.

To proceed: Click "More info" and then click "Run anyway".

3

Launch and Dictate

Double-click Toro.exe. Look for the Toro icon in your taskbar system tray. Focus your text area, press and hold "Ctrl + Space", speak naturally, and release!
